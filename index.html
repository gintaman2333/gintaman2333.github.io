Recipe #1 Read Bean Loaf

  • Time: 3 hrs total
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients
(in grams)		 Quantity
ver1		 Quantity
ver2		 Quantity
ver3
bread flour 250 270 300
sweetened
read bean		 150 160 170
dry yeast 3 3.5 5
water 130 150 150
butter 20 20 20
sugar 30 30 35
salt 3 4 4

Step 1: Mix everything except butter.

Step 2: Mix butter into the dough.

Step 3: Roll the dough into a rectangle, position the red bean spread.

Step 4: Fold and roll 4 times, cut into 3 strands, braid them.

Step 5: Proof for 1 hr. Use the oven (35 degrees?)

Step 6: Set oven temperature to 180~200 degrees, preheat 10 mins. Bake for 35~42 mins.

Recipe #2 Pad Thai

  • Time: 1.5 hrs total
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients (in grams) Quantity
vermicelli 350
green bean spout 1 box
chinese chive some
fried tofu some
egg 3
chicken/prawn prepare some
crushed peanuts some
pad thai sauce 3 large spoons

Step 1: Soak vermicelli in hot water for 1 hr. (If not, boil them for 5 mins before stir fry.) Dry them.

Step 2: Stir fry all the veggies and meat.

Step 3: Stir fry vermicelli and add pad thai sauce. Add some sugar too.

Step 4: Mix with prepared toppings

Step 5: Sprinkle the peanuts.

Recipe #3 Pizza

  • Time: 1 hrs total
  • Serves: 3 or 6
Ingredients
(in grams)		 Quantity
ver1		 Quantity
ver2
regular flour 250 500
dry yeast 4 6.6
salt 3 6
sugar 12 25
water 120 250
oil* 12~18 25~35
meat&veggie some some
cheese some some

Step 1: Mix everything to make dough.

Step 2: Roll it into a disk, use a fork to make tiny holes. Proof for 10~20 mins

Step 3: Lay toppings. Veggies first, then meat, finally cheese.

Step 4: Set oven to 200 degrees. Preheat for 5 mins.

Step 5: Cook pizza for 12~15 mins.
